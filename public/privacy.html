Privacy Policy

This policy explains, in general terms, how the hosted MekBay service may collect, generate, use, disclose, retain, and otherwise process account information, cloud content, local browser data, and service telemetry.

Last updated: April 5, 2026 Applies to the hosted MekBay service

Cloud content may be used publicly

Anything you sync, submit, or publish through the hosted service may be accessed, copied, adapted, disclosed, and publicly featured by the service operators for hosted-service and related project purposes.

High-level description

This policy describes the kinds of information the hosted service may process at a high level. Internal systems, storage methods, and retention workflows may change over time.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the hosted MekBay service operated for the project. It does not automatically apply to forks, private deployments, or third-party instances.

2. Information We Process

Depending on how you use the hosted service, MekBay may collect, receive, generate, infer, or otherwise process categories of information such as:

  • account identifiers and account state, such as UUIDs, public IDs, linked-provider identifiers, sign-in status, and related timestamps;
  • profile and authentication-provider data made available by sign-in providers, such as names, handles, email addresses, avatars, verification indicators, and similar account fields;
  • content you sync, submit, upload, create, import, store, publish, or otherwise make available through the hosted service, including forces, operations, organizations, tags, subscriptions, searches, notes, metadata, media, and derived outputs;
  • local browser and device data used by the application, including caches, offline data, generated artifacts, local copies, preferences, and working state;
  • service, network, device, request, and diagnostic information, such as IP addresses or other routing information, timestamps, error records, request and session identifiers, usage state, connection events, abuse-prevention signals, and security logs; and
  • communications, reports, support requests, moderation records, and other information you send to the project.

3. How We Use Information

MekBay may use this information for any legitimate purpose related to the hosted service or related MegaMek and MekBay project activity, including to:

  • operate, maintain, secure, debug, improve, test, support, and administer the hosted service;
  • authenticate users, link accounts, restore access, synchronize state, and manage service features;
  • investigate abuse, fraud, security incidents, policy violations, and technical failures;
  • review, moderate, analyze, adapt, reproduce, disclose, archive, and otherwise use hosted content;
  • create demos, examples, showcases, documentation, streams, screenshots, changelogs, promotional materials, community highlights, and other public or internal materials; and
  • comply with law, enforce policies, protect rights, and prepare for project continuity, transfer, archival, or shutdown.

4. Cloud Content Is Not Confidential

Content made available through the hosted service should be treated as non-confidential and potentially public. Hosted content may be accessed, reviewed, copied, modified, adapted, disclosed, displayed, published, archived, and shared by the hosted service operators and their designees for service operation and related project purposes.

This can include public-facing uses such as examples, screenshots, issue discussions, demos, documentation, streams, social posts, showcases, and promotional materials. Do not use the hosted service for secrets, highly sensitive personal data, regulated information, or any material you are not prepared to have disclosed or retained.

5. How We May Share Or Disclose Information

MekBay may share or disclose information, including hosted content, whenever we consider it appropriate for the hosted service or related project activity, including:

  • to operators, maintainers, moderators, contributors, contractors, vendors, and infrastructure providers involved with the project or the hosted service;
  • to authentication providers, integration partners, and other third parties involved in account access or feature delivery;
  • to other users or to the public through sharing features, community discussions, examples, showcases, documentation, demos, streams, screenshots, and promotional materials;
  • in connection with audits, investigations, legal process, law-enforcement requests, safety issues, or protection of rights, property, systems, or users; and
  • in connection with project restructuring, archival, transfer, mirror hosting, or succession of service operations.

6. Local Browser And Device Storage

The application may store significant working data locally in your browser or on your device, including caches, generated artifacts, drawings, preferences, offline copies, and device-local copies of account or synced content. This local storage may improve performance, support offline behavior, and restore your workspace on the same device.

Local browser or device data is separate from server-side hosted data. Clearing local storage, using logout, or removing site data from a device affects that device but does not necessarily delete server-side account records, hosted content, logs, backups, archives, or linked authentication-provider relationships.

7. Retention

MekBay may retain information for as long as we consider useful, necessary, or appropriate for the hosted service or related project purposes, including operation, debugging, abuse prevention, historical recordkeeping, backups, archives, legal compliance, dispute resolution, and enforcement of project rules and terms.

Deletion requests, logout actions, local browser clearing, account changes, or service shutdown do not guarantee immediate or complete removal from all systems. Copies may remain in caches, backups, logs, archives, screenshots, mirrors, public materials, and derivative works for an indefinite period, subject to applicable law.

8. Your Choices

OAuth sign-in is optional. You may stop using the hosted service, clear local browser data, or use device-level controls to limit local storage. You may also contact the project through the channels below regarding account-access or data-removal questions.

Except as required by applicable law, MekBay does not guarantee deletion, correction, portability, confidentiality, access rights, restoration, response times, or a fixed retention schedule for hosted content or hosted account records.

9. Children's Privacy

The hosted service is not directed to children and is not intended to collect information from children where doing so would require parental consent under applicable law. If you are not old enough to use the hosted service lawfully where you live, do not use it.

10. Changes To This Policy

MekBay may update this Privacy Policy at any time by posting a revised version on this page. The updated policy becomes effective when posted unless a later effective date is stated. Continued use of the hosted service after the effective date of a revised policy constitutes acknowledgment of the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about privacy, account access, or data-removal requests can be sent to the MegaMek project maintainers through the MekBay issue tracker or the MegaMek Discord.

This policy applies to the hosted MekBay service. If you run your own fork or private deployment, you are responsible for publishing your own privacy disclosures.
